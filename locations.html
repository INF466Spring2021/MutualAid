Capital Region Corona Support

Albany, New York

About: We are Capital District residents turning to one another for support during the Coronavirus pandemic and the associated economic fallout. There are so many ways to get involved! We are working on a website, but in the meantime please visit our welcome page. Click here for Capital Region Corona Support welcome page and to:

- Learn more about mutual aid and regional resources

- Start a "neighborhood pod" (groups of neighbors supporting each other) or register an existing one

- Request or offer help

- Volunteer to support with coordination efforts or to translate

- Offer classes, activities, or resources to the community

Community Care Planning: Main Hub

Chatham, New York

About: A central hub for connecting and plugging into various self-organizing groups, using Facebook infrastructure. The community here centers from Columbia Co. NY with members from nearby counties as well. To channel and direct everyone's interests and needs, we have working groups (purpose and topics of interest--for all folks from the bigger region of the Hudson Valley) and community pods (smaller geographical units).
