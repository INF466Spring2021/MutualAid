Capital Region Corona Support
Albany, New York
About: We are Capital District residents turning to one another for support during the Coronavirus pandemic and the associated economic fallout. There are so many ways to get involved! We are working on a website, but in the meantime please visit our welcome page () to:
  1. Learn more about mutual aid and regional resources
  2. Start a "neighborhood pod" (groups of neighbors supporting each other) or register an existing one
  3. Request or offer help
  4. Volunteer to support with coordination efforts or to translate
  5. offer classes, activities, or resources to the community
