Do I have to incorporate my mutual aid society right away?

No. If while operating your mutual aid society you decide to incorporate you can do so despite being active prior to incorporating. However the benefits of incorporation will not apply retroactively to activities prior to incorporation.

Do I have to have a Board of Directors?

If you are incorporated then you will need to have a board of directors. If you are not incorporated then you will not be required to have one.
